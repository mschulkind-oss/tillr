# Name Candidates for "lifecycle"

> **Current working name:** lifecycle
> **What the tool does:** A human-in-the-loop project management CLI+web tool for agentic software development. It sits between humans and AI agents, steering work through iteration cycles.

---

## Top 5 Recommendations

| Rank | Name | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Tiller** | Evokes steering/guiding without being overloaded. A tiller is the lever that steers a boat — perfect metaphor for a human steering AI agents. No major conflicts in dev tooling. Short, memorable, pleasant to type. |
| 2 | **Bearing** | Navigation concept — your bearing is your direction of travel. Implies keeping things on course. No major dev tool conflicts. Works as both noun and verb context ("get your bearings"). |
| 3 | **Relay** | Captures the handoff nature between human and agent. A relay race requires coordination and trust between runners. No major conflicts. Clean, short, widely understood. |
| 4 | **Cadence** | Rhythm of iteration cycles. Implies regularity and human-set pace. Some minor conflicts (Cadence workflow engine by Uber), but the workflow engine is less prominent now. |
| 5 | **Turnstile** | A controlled gate — things pass through one at a time, with human approval. Captures the checkpoint/approval nature well. Cloudflare has a CAPTCHA product called Turnstile, but different domain. |
